The Platform

An enterprise field-intelligence platform built for professional organisations.

Field teams are the operational backbone of organisations in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and commercial distribution. Their activity — visits, evaluations, account development, commercial wins — drives revenue, shapes relationships, and generates market intelligence. But that activity is rarely captured consistently or reliably.

FieldIQ solves this. It gives field professionals a structured way to log what they do, and gives managers real-time visibility into territory performance, participation, and execution — without replacing the systems organisations already use.

FieldIQ is not a CRM. It is a field-intelligence platform designed to complement existing business systems by improving participation, visibility, and accountability — working alongside CRM and ERP infrastructure rather than replacing it.
Why FIQ Exists

Organisations depend on consistent field participation. Participation depends on recognition.

Field teams avoid logging systems not because they lack discipline, but because those systems are slow, disconnected, and offer no visible return for the effort. The result is incomplete data, unreliable reporting, and managers working with a fraction of the intelligence they need.

FIQ is intended to address this directly. As the participation and recognition layer of the FieldIQ ecosystem, FIQ is designed to recognise verified, consistent contribution — making meaningful field activity visible and acknowledged within the platform.

FIQ is a utility token intended for use within the FieldIQ ecosystem to support participation programmes and recognise contribution from verified field activity.

01
Recognise verified participation
FIQ is intended to acknowledge field professionals whose activity is verified within the FieldIQ platform — creating a transparent record of contribution that benefits both the individual and the organisation.
02
Reduce administrative resistance
When contribution is acknowledged, participation improves. FieldIQ is designed to make logging fast and meaningful — and FIQ is intended to reinforce that the effort is seen and valued.
03
Support long-term ecosystem alignment
FIQ is designed for longevity. Fixed supply, no post-deployment minting, and a structured allocation model reflect a long-term view of ecosystem participation rather than short-term distribution.
